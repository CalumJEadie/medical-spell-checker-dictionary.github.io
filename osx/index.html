OSX

Solution 1

  • Download US-English-Medical-Dictionary.dic.
  • Open US-English-Medical-Dictionary.dic in TextEdit.
  • Right click on Finder. Click "Go to Folder...". Go to the folder "~/Library/Spelling". Open LocalDictionary in TextEdit.
  • Copy everything from US-English-Medical-Dictionary.dic into LocalDictionary. Save LocalDictionary.
  • You can now trash US-English-Medical-Dictionary.dic
  • Restart your computer.
  • Done!

Solution 2

  • Download Installer.
  • Run Installer.
  • Done!
